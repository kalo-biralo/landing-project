This website is for the blue lock anime

This website has some simple info about the anime and manga Blue Lock. Blue lock is about football. Sign up if you wanna be a part of the blue lock community.

Favorite Characters
Isagi Yoichi: Isagi is the protagonist of the manga Blue Lock who dreams of becoming the best striker in the world. His weapon is his football IQ and amazing spatial awareness.
Seishiro Nagi: Seishiro is widely considered a genius. He plays with minimal effort until he loses to Isagi and develops a new love for the game. His weapon is his aerial control and fakes.
Meguru Bachira: Bachira is a natural dribbler who has an insane love for football. He plays the game freely and has a very carefree charater similar to his playing style.
Rin Itoshi: Rin is probably one of the best players in all of Blue Lock and is one of the few players above and beyond Isagi. Isagi aims to completely beat Rin one day.
